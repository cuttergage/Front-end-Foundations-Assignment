About Me

I have spent the last 16 years of my life focusing on playing, writing, and recording music. While my passion in that area will continue, I have had increased excitement in recent months around the idea and opportunity to learn a new technical skill in software engineering.

What feels appealing about coding is the intersection between technique and design. I am detail-oriented and thus appreciate structure and accuracy. In tandem to that, I deeply enjoy having an outlet for creativity to express beauty in a way that brings a platform above and beyond what is simply functional. The field of web development allows me the opportunity to think strategically to accomplish objectives within a creative environment for both front-end and back-end work. By the end of my training, I will be able to offer clients high-quality work in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and PostgreSQL.

An additional value and priority I hold is the commitment to work with people - whether that be clients, users, or fellow developers. If you're looking for a new junior developer to add to your team, please don't hesitate to reach out! I would be thrilled to have the chance to collaborate with other experienced coders who are passionate about their craft that I could learn from.
